Taste the Future

As Panda Express grows and defines American Chinese cuisine, we're constantly exploring new flavors and elements of design. Here's a peek at what’s coming your way from our Chinese Kitchen.

Food + Drink

Wok-fired dishes will always be the heart of our menu, but we’re exploring innovative ways to let our guests customize their Panda experience, with build-your-own wraps and salads, as well as house-made sauces and garnishes. Want an Orange Chicken wrap with spicy sambal and green papaya slaw? Coming right up.

Our Panda Tea Bar, a modern tea house experience with a wide variety of hand-crafted drinks featuring fresh fruits, fun mix-ins and imported teas, is on hand to quench your thirst after all that good eating

Look + Feel

In addition to perfecting our menu, we're also dedicated to refining the details. Everything from ambiance to associate uniforms and packaging are tinkered with until an ideal prototype is landed upon. Our newest restaurants have an abundance of natural light and festive communal seating in order for guests to feel perfectly at home in our family’s Chinese kitchen. Equally important to us is the comfort of our associates, the heart of Panda Express, which means designing uniforms that move, breathe and look great.

Sustainability

Panda Restaurant Group understands that the future of our restaurants can only be as bright as the planet on which they reside, so we’re reducing our carbon footprint and use of natural resources through a number of efforts including commercially compostable packaging, oil recycling and energy efficient buildings whenever and wherever possible.
